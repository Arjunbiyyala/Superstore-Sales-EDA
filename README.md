Superstore Sales EDA

This project focuses on exploratory data analysis of a retail Superstore Sales dataset. The goal was to understand sales trends, identify seasonal patterns, and generate business insights using Python.

Project Overview

The dataset contains 9800 rows and 18 columns including order details, product categories, customer information, and sales values.

In this project, I performed data cleaning, converted date columns to proper datetime format, and analyzed yearly and monthly sales performance.

Key Findings

• November is the highest revenue generating month.
• Sales increase significantly in the last quarter of the year (October to December).
• 2018 recorded the highest annual sales among all years.
• California is the top revenue generating state.
• Phones is the highest selling sub-category.

Business Insights

Based on the analysis, businesses should increase inventory before Q4, launch premium products before peak months, and invest more in marketing around September to maximize November sales.
